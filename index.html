Come Ride with Us

Have you ever longed to try riding a horse?

To feel the exhilaration as you soar through the air over a jump or thunder across a field feeling the wind against your face?


Here's your chance.


  • From absolute beginner to seasoned soul, you'll love it.
  • We cater for lessons, treks, livery and if course fun competitions and parties
  • Make friends and take a break from reality on one of our magnificent animals.
Ready to take the first step?

Welcome to the Southern Cross Equestrian Centre.


We are a fun family run centre that has been established for more than 20 years.

The centre is located in a beautiful area of Bray, Wicklow and has spectatular views while being very convienent to the N11.

We cater for everyone, kids, adults, beginners and advanced.

We have classes , ride-outs, livery, leagues and camps.

We also have mounts to suit everyone even the most nervous and we take safety very seriously.

We are fully approved by AIRE.

We also take our fun very seriously as you can see from out Gallery section.

So what are you waiting for?
