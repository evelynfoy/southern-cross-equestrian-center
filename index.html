Come Ride with Us

Have you ever longed to try riding a horse?

To feel the exhilaration as you soar through the air over a jump or thunder across a field feeling the wind against your face?

Here's your chance.

  • From absolute beginner to seasoned soul, you'll love it.
  • We cater for lessons, treks, livery and if course fun competitions and parties
  • Make friends and take a break from reality on one of our magnificent animals.
Ready to take the first step?

Welcome to the Southern Cross Equestrian Centre.

We are a fun family run centre that has been established for more than 20 years.

The centre is located in a beautiful area of Bray, Wicklow and has spectatular views while being very convienent to the N11.

We cater for everyone, kids, adults, beginners and advanced.

We have classes , ride-outs, livery, leagues and camps.

We also have mounts to suit everyone even the most nervous and we take safety very seriously.

We are fully approved by AIRE .

We also take our fun very seriously as you can see from out Gallery section.

So what are you waiting for?

Kids

We love welcoming kids to the Southern Cross.

We cater for all levels and ages.

They can ride their own pony or make friends with ours.

For complete beginners we recommend they start out with lessons.

Then they can participate in ride-outs.

If they are interested they can join our pony club.

Adults

The Southern Cross is not just for Kids

We pride ourselves on making this an enyoyable experiance for everyone.

We cater for all levels of experiance.

You can ride your own mount or make friends with ours.

For complete beginners we recommend you start out with lessons.

Then you can participate in ride-outs.

If you are interested you can join our riding club.

Livery

We provide livery for your horse or pony for very reasonable rates.

  • We have a large livery yard with access to grass areas.
  • We provide feed and hay.
  • We provide mucking out and grooming services.
  • You can avail of out extensive facilites.
  • There is a separate livery tack area with lockers.
  • You can be assured your animal will receive expert care.
